Star Wars: Death Star Rescue

Scene 1: Prison Cell

Luke enters Princess Leia's cell wearing stormtrooper armor.

LEIA: Aren't you a little short to be a stormtrooper?
LUKE: What? Oh... the uniform. I'm Luke Skywalker. I'm here to rescue you.
LEIA: You're who?

Scene 2: Detention Corridor

The heroes are trapped. Blaster fire fills the corridor with smoke.

HAN: Can't get out that way.
LEIA: This is some rescue. Didn't you have a plan for getting out?
HAN: He's the brains, sweetheart.
LUKE: Well, I didn't...

Stormtroopers advance from both ends of the corridor.

Scene 3: Control Room

R2-D2 and C-3PO hide in the control room.

THREEPIO: We're doomed!
ARTOO: Beep boop whistle!

Scene 4: Garbage Chute

Leia takes charge as stormtroopers close in.

LEIA: Somebody has to save our skins. Into the garbage chute, wise guy.
HAN: Get in there you big furry oaf! I don't care what you smell!
HAN: Wonderful girl! Either I'm going to kill her or I'm beginning to like her.

One by one, they jump into the garbage chute and disappear.

Scene 5: Imperial Command

On the Death Star bridge, two figures sense a disturbance.

VADER: I sense something... a presence I haven't felt since...
TARKIN: The Jedi are extinct. Their fire has gone out of the universe.
